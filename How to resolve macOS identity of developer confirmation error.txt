How to make BrickWarrior run on macOS when it complains "Can't be opened because
the identity of the developer cannot be confirmed"

* Put BrickWarrior.app in your home folder (the parent of Documents / Desktop)
* Open up the Terminal app
* Type: xattr -r -d com.apple.quarantine BrickWarrior.app
* Press enter
* Close Terminal

Apologies that this is necessary, but Apple wants to charge $150/year to
developers to 'sign' their apps to make these warnings go away.
